The family in one paragraph

Renée (2017) and Léon (2019) were born in Leuven. Their father's families run to the Flemish coast: the Bundervoets, documented to Evergem farmers of the early 1700s and then dock-workers of Oostende; the Dekeysers and Devriendts of Oostende, one branch of which took wartime refuge in London. Deep collateral lines (Vanstechelman) reach back to the 1660s. Their mother Dorien's families belong to the Brussels periphery — Thumas, Janssens, Vandenhoeck and Van Craenenbroeck of Kraainem and Zaventem. Two very different corners of Belgium, joined in these two children.
