# Sources & document traces

A running index of the primary and secondary sources behind the tree, with stable
identifiers so any claim can be re-checked. Images of key acts are saved under
`docs/sources/`. Narrative discovery notes live in `docs/research-log.md`.

Confidence key: **doc** = seen in a primary act/image or an authoritative index;
**sup** = single member tree, not yet checked against the act; **fam** = family testimony.

## De Keyser branch

### S1 — Édouard Dekeyser's 1946 Oostende remarriage act  ★ wall-breaker
- **Proves:** Édouard Dekeyser's parents = **Desiderius De Keyser × Maria Theresia Van den Broeck**;
  his birthplace = **Hamme (Oost-Vlaanderen)**, birth 12 Nov 1876; his 1st marriage to
  **Louisa Maria Bocklandt** ended in **divorce (~1923, Rechtbank van eersten aanleg Brugge)**;
  he **remarried Leontine Schreel** in Oostende on 9 May 1946.
- **Collection:** FamilySearch — "Belgium, West Flanders, Civil Registration, 1582-1950"
  (België, Arr. Brugge, Burgerlijke Stand 1921-1950: Oostende, Huwelijkscertificaten 1946, akte nr. 81).
- **Index record:** https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:X9R6-DJNV
- **Image:** https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/3:1:3QHN-LQKQ-R48T-B (image 32 of 344)
- **Local asset:** `docs/sources/edouard-dekeyser-1946-marriage-oostende.jpg`
- **Confidence:** doc · accessed 2026-07-22
